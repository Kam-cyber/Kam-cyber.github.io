My visit on Barrio Antiguo

07/10/2024


I visit the Barrio Antiguo in Monterrey, Nuevo Le√≥n. I'm not much for going out to explore new places, but honestly this place captivated me.


Perhaps it is the most bohemian place in Monterrey, because you see art everywhere, in the houses, restaurants, items they offer, in the music, it is as a magical place.


The Old Town has managed to maintain the essence of "old", this through the colorful architecture present in each of the houses that surround the streets, side by side.


This is a place where you can spend the afternoon, walking through the streets, surrounding yourself with colors and allegorical music. In my experience I felt like I was traveling to the past, getting lost among so many colors, the smell of the restaurants as you pass by, the music that accompanies your trip, it is something unmatched.


I do not leave aside the beautiful crafts that the small businesses sell in that place, too many worthy crafts to appreciate. Each one representing Mexico in all its splendor.

Places where I ate in Barrio Antiguo!


I can't deny that the food at this place is so delicious, full of flavor, colors and extremely traditional. Some of the foods I was able to find are the typical enchiladas, entomatadas, drowned tacos, mole, etc. Each of these meals is an explosion of flavor on the palate!


Some of the places I visited are: La Sirena Embarazada, Trece Lunas, Matarile and ESTAQUEARDE. I loved these places because they sell delicious food, and also desserts, that leave you wanting to eat more.


Personally, I would go to that place countless times and never get tired of it, I would explore the place with excitement as if I had never seen it. If you like adventures and spend hours immersed in a place, this is the perfect place. And believe me, you won't get bored, like me.
